I'm Nick Jacobs, a full stack software developer from Milwaukee, WI.

I have traveled down many paths in life before deciding this one.

I started working in the service industry, and that occupied 15 years of my work life. I went to university to become a teacher, and switched my focus to political science. Eventually I had the realization that it was not for me.

I continued working in the service industry for a few years, but my passion was always with tech. Ever since I was a child, I was fascinated with computers and technology. I ran an online gaming community, going as far as building a LLC, renting servers and maintaining a forum site. I severly missed it as I grew older. Eventually the pandemic hit and I decided to take a risk, leave my job and attend a coding bootcamp.

The amount of raw information was intimidating at first. But as I battle onward, it only solidifies my desire and passion for working in the industry. No day is mundane when accepting new challenges.
